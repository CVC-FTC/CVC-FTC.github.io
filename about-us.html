About Us

We are a robotics club based at Comberton village College in Cambridgeshire, UK

Since 2018, we have competed in the First Tech Challenge in the UK, with various degrees of success.

In the 2019/20 season we won the inspire award, which would have allowed us to advance to nationals, however, COVID had other ideas.

This season(24/25), we have advanced to the Nationals with a Wild Card. Where we will Compete in the Copperbox arena in the Olympic Park.
