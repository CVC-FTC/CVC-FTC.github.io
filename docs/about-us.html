About Us

Building robots, building futures since 2018

We are a robotics club based at Comberton Village College in Cambridgeshire, UK. Since 2018, we have competed in the FIRST Tech Challenge in the UK, achieving various degrees of success.

In the 2019/20 season, we won the Inspire Award, which would have qualified us for nationals, but the event was canceled due to COVID-19.

Last season (2024/25), we advanced to the Nationals with a Wild Card and competed at the CopperBox Arena in the Olympic Park, London.
